# Product Guidelines: Learn from Buffett

## Core Principles
The platform's design and user experience are guided by three core principles: **Authoritative Insight**, **Modern Clarity**, and **Contextual Intelligence**.

## Prose Style & Tone
- **Tone:** **Insightful & Authoritative.** The writing should reflect the wisdom of Warren Buffett while being accessible and professional.
- **Language:** English and Chinese. Clear, concise, and focused on educational value for financial concepts.
- **Style:** Avoid financial jargon without explanation; the AI should be the primary bridge to understanding complex terms.

## Branding & Visual Identity
- **Visual Style:** **Modern Clean (White/Black).** A minimalist, high-contrast palette ensures maximum readability for long-form reading.
- **Typography:** Serif for readability in letters, Sans-serif for navigation and controls to maintain a modern feel.
- **Colors:** Dominant black and white, with subtle accents to highlight interactive elements and active states.

## UX & UI Guidelines
- **Mobile First:** Prioritize a seamless reading and interaction experience on mobile devices.
- **Contextual Insights (Feature-Rich):** Provide immediate, on-demand access to AI analysis and personal notes directly within the reading context.
- **Seamless Interactivity:** Highlighting and note-taking should feel integrated and non-disruptive to the reading flow.
- **Information Architecture:** A clean, flat hierarchy to ensure users can quickly navigate between different years and letters.
