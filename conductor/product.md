# Product Guide: Learn from Buffett

## Vision
A modern, responsive platform designed for active investors to read, study, and derive insights from Warren Buffett's shareholder letters. The goal is to make these foundational investment resources more accessible and digestible through high-quality translations, AI-powered analysis, and interactive study tools.

## Initial Concept
A web application providing segmented, bilingual access to Berkshire Hathaway's shareholder letters, enhanced by AI for clarifying complex financial concepts.

## Target Audience
- **Active Investors:** Seeking deep insights from Buffett's letters for their own investment practice.
- **Value Investing Students:** Studying the core principles of Buffett and Munger.

## Core Features (MVP)
1. **Bilingual Reading:** Side-by-side English and Chinese translation for every paragraph to aid comprehension.
2. **AI Deep Analysis:** Multi-turn AI dialogue to clarify complex financial concepts and historical context.
3. **Interactive Highlighting & Notes:** Ability to highlight key passages and attach personal notes for future reference.
4. **Social Sharing:** Generate and share beautiful insight cards on social media to spread investment wisdom.

## Success Metrics
- **Active Readers (Retention):** Focused on the number of daily and weekly active users engaging with the letters.
- **AI Engagement:** Measuring how effectively the AI assists users in understanding the content.
- **Growth:** Tracking the sharing of insight cards and user acquisition through social channels.
